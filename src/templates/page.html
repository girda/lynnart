«I Dream my paint, and then i paint my dream.» Vincent van gogh

"As an artist, I perceive art as a space of absolute freedom to express myself. In each painting I strive to embody a surreal fantasy world where reality and imagination become a natural extension of each other.

In this space without the laws of logic and physics it becomes possible to realize any ideas, even the most incredible ones, through amusing and bizarre forms and symbols.

Awaken your imagination and become a wizard for a while! Follow me into this “rabbit hole” to figure out how far imagination can lead us."
